Getting Help with ntop...

Please understand that ntop is open source software, and that all help is self-help, that is provided by other users to the best of their interest and ability.

The primary source of help are the ntop mailing lists. There are three, ntop (users),ntop-dev (coding), and ntop-misc (misc issues related to ntop). Anybody can read the message archives, but only members can post messages.

Please note that the mail lists are not indexed. However, they are spidered by search engines and you can sometimes find the message you're looking for via a google search: "site:listgateway.unipi.it+ntop+xxxxx" . gmane has a search engine, at search.gmane.org. Please note that this search doesn't look inside attachments and doesn't always have older messages indexed.
